Other Technical Skills

  • Ancillary / peripheral skills
  • required for successful open source leadership
  • help you stand out professionally

Bug reports

 

A lot harder than it sounds

  • Reproduce bugs
  • Describe problems in detail
  • Look at big picture, consider interactions

Documentation

 

Desperately needed by most projects

  • Requires in-depth knowledge
  • Requires discipline

Packaging

 

Remember all those Linux distributions?

  • Ease installation
  • key component of automation
  • Home-grown apps installed within a company

Support

 

Dealing with people

  • Deal with customer-facing issues by interacting directly with customers
  • Realtime or asynchronously: both are hard
  • Language and culture issues always come into play
  • Meet users where they are, not where you are

Testing

 

Committment to quality

  • Demonstrate your committment to solving problems the right way
  • Break down problems into testable pieces
  • Prove your work

UX

 

Not just user interfaces

  • Think about user experience in important ways
  • Not just for websites or mobile apps
  • Do you enjoy SELinux, or writing Postfix configs?
